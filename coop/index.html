Talk Shoppe
Co-Op

Meet the Talk Shoppe Co-Op! From time to time, Talk Shoppe engages one or more of our 'co-operatives' to work with us on projects. All of our co-operatives have years of experience in the field of market research and boast a unique set of qualifications.





Reyna Hohagen

Reyna is a trilingual: English, French and Spanish, consumer insights expert and analytic thinker. Her career started over 15 years ago as a Hispanic and children’s market specialist at C&R Research, in Chicago and developed in Paris, France as a multicultural, qualitative research analyst.

Mike Ostenson

Mike has 15 years of experience working in advertising and market research. He started his career in Account Planning at Leo Burnett, and spent the better part of a decade at Hall & Partners in Chicago where he headed up the Target, Samsung, Bissell, Caribou Coffee, and Absolut accounts. He's an astute qualitative researcher that combines creativity with discipline, aiming to deliver actionable insights to his clients.

Jennifer Rivas

Jennifer's knowledge of the Hispanic market involves close familiarity of the demographic with a deep understanding of the culture across acculturation levels. A native of Los Angeles, Jennifer is bilingual, bicultural and biliterate, skills that allow her to effectively engage consumers in both English and Spanish. Her career in Hispanic research began at Los Angeles’ largest Spanish-language newspaper, La Opinión, more than seven years ago where she focused on how, and what, influenced Hispanics and how they consumed media.

Jon McNeill

Jon is a trained ethnographer who has spent the past few years pushing the intersection between research and film. Equally comfortable with product and brand development, Jon's expertise covers both in-person and online methodologies in multiple categories such as Automotive, Online, Beverage, and Apparel.
