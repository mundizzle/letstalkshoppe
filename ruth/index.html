PartnerRuth Behr

Ruth Behr's journey into the world of research started in 1997 in Minneapolis where she worked as a research analyst at Haworth on Target in a multi-faceted media and primary research role. Her exposure to and fascination with media research at Haworth eventually led her to a management- level media planning/strategy role at Universal McCann working on a portfolio of Nestle brands. There, she was exposed to account and communications planning and eventually moved into a strategy role at agencies such as Carat and Zenith doing primary and media research for clients like Adidas, Gap, Hyundai/Kia, New Line and 20th Century Fox/Searchlight. Her love of research led her to a Director role at Nielsen where she spent a few years working on custom solutions with a focus on profiling and linking marketing targets with campaign planning. Clients included Microsoft, Yahoo, Hershey’s, Kraft, P&G, Universal Pictures, and Focus Features. She brings an end-to-end perspective of campaign planning– from identifying and profiling the most important consumers and how best to target them with communications all the way through to measuring the holistic impact of cross-platform campaigns. Ruth brings 14 years of qualitative and quantitative research experience to Talk Shoppe.
