PartnerJennifer Dyke

While studying economics at university in London, Jenny took comfort in the black and white guidance that data can provide. However, over her career, she has come to realize that this is only one piece of the puzzle when it comes to providing guidance to move clients forward. Although she heads up our quantitative division, Jenny frequently works alongside the qualitative team on hybrid qualitative/quantitative studies to ensure that our research provides the full picture. She has eleven years of brand and communications research experience, including three years specialized within PR.
